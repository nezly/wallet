Privacy Policy - Nezly Online Wallet

This policy may be updated or revised without notice. It is the responsibility of the user to stay informed about privacy policy changes.

The Nezly Wallet does track your actions on this client.

The Nezly Wallet does not store cookies and the website does not contain any analytics scripts.

Nezly developers never see your private keys.

However, the Nezly wallet is hosted on GitHub and AWS. They may and do have their own tracking systems on their servers. Those services have their own privacy policies and they are not covered by this privacy policy.

While the Nezly Wallet does not track you, this does not mean your actions are private. Take note of other privacy issues that may affect you:

  • Stellar is a public ledger. Anyone can see anything that happens on their network.
  • Your inflation vote is publicly visible.
  • Your computer might be compromised.
  • The Nezly Wallet website might be compromised.
